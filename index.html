The delectable aroma of freshly baked cupcakes with the warmth of the base and the cool sweet icing can be a mouth watering experience. Baking lovers craft the cupcakes with the nourishing touch of the sweet scent of flavours and savouring taste. And we all would kill at some point to feel the taste in our mouth.

However, this unimaginable experienced is destroyed then we would be quite disappointed. How would you feel if you are craving badly for cupcakes, but when you order and they arrive, the cakes are squished in their containers? I’m sure you would hate it. And so will the bakers who bake these cakes with their care and love.

The reason is that the boxes that you’re using for the packaging are frail and unsecured. They are not supporting to the cake and are not sturdy enough to hold them. Hence, what you should use instead is our strong, well-developed, and solid auto pop up boxes.

We started NIKOZO, a cupcake, and bakery goods packaging company, with the mission to rejoice those you want to share your sweet meals with and spread happiness to them through these delightful treats.

Moreover, we know how our home-bakers make their cupcakes with the utmost care, so we too make our packages so that they support the cupcakes with the same care and love so that you enjoy them with their warm delicious taste.
