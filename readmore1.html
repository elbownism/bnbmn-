Construction:

The most central difference between an auto pop up box and a fold-and-lock box is in their construction. Auto pop up, as the name suggests, are set automatically and do not require much time to set them manually. However, fold and lock boxes require that they are assembled manually. Therefore, a fold ad lock box might take some extra time to open as compared to an auto pop up box. This is because you have to fold and lock the box step by step and that could be time-taking. Thus, auto pop is assembled automatically within seconds while fold and lock takes quite some extra time.

Dimensions:

Another difference that you might find in an auto pop up box and a fold and lock box is that they might have different dimensions. This doesn’t mean that they cannot have the same dimensions. However, our auto pop boxes comes in the standard size of the cupcakes. Fold and lock, on the other hand, are usually customized. This means that they come in different size, shape, and volume. Our boxes are white cupcake boxes with ribbon and a thank you card and they come in a standard size. Insertion diameter = 2.5 inches Height = 4 inches Length = 13.4 inches Width = 10 inches

Locks:

Lastly, another difference between auto pop up and fold and lock box is between the locks of the boxes. As told in the construction of the boxes, an auto pop has an automatic construction while a fold and lock has a manual construction. Hence, auto pop up box does not have any sort of locks to do the packaging. It is simply assembled without any inconvenience and very easy to open as well. A fold and lock box, on the other hand is construction with locks. So, you have to lock the card board sheet in the other side of the box to assemble it in a box shape.
