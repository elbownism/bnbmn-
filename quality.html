Sturdiness of material:

The material that we use for the packaging in our boxes, it is strong and firm. It is very supportive for the cupcakes. It holds them firmly and does not allow them to squish and be destroyed. Hence, the strong material provides a sturdy support to the contents inside. Hence, our product is durable and remain unaffected during changed situations. So, if the cupcakes are to be taken away or carried for delivery and this travelling is a little tough one, the cupcakes will remain unharmed in majority incidents. Hence, they are a good choice if you want your cupcakes to remain safe during the journey.

Easily assembled:

One of the most significant virtues that exist in our auto pop up cupcake boxes is that they are very easily assembled. Like we discussed that they are automatically assembled as they have no folds or locks but can be simply accumulated. Thus, not so much effort is put into the building up of these boxes as they can be basically attached within just a few seconds. This can save lots of time for the bakers as they don’t have to worry about the time of assembling a cupcake packaging box. Hence, they can effortlessly assemble the boxes very conveniently.

Viewing Windows:

The top section of the box consists of a transparent window through which you can see the contents inside the box. Hence, you can see that how fancy your cupcakes are and how they look through that box. This provides a great way to display the cupcakes and its decorations to grab some eyeballs. Therefore, this viewing window adds to the beauty of the box. The box itself is white and simple. It seems very elegant and with a ribbon and our special thank you note, it beautifies our boxes in a perfectly elite piece. This will not just boost up your sales with pretty well display but also gives an option to check the cupcakes if they get ruined. It is like keeping an eye on your cupcakes until delivery.

Insert separation:

As mentioned earlier, the insertion diameter of the box is 2.5 inches. This is quite well space for a standard size cupcake. This allows the cupcake to not get squished and remain in its place supported nicely by the walls of the segmented insertion. There is 6 of these insertion spaces in the box and so you can put 6 cupcakes in one box. Hence, there is a lot of space in the box to keep the cupcakes without causing any destruction to them like them spattered against the walls of the box. Hence, you can with no trouble place the cupcakes in the box.
